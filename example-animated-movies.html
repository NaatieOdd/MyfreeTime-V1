Jujutsu Kaisen 0 by:Gege Akutami

Jujutsu Kaisen 0 is an japanese animated dark fantasy film from 2021, based on a mangaserie by the same name. The story follows Yuta Okkotsu and his troubles being cursed. Yuta was noticed by a school that specializes in training people with curses, and this way he finds his mentor Satoru Gojo. But that isn't the end of the story a dangerous villian appears trying to steal Yuta's powers.
