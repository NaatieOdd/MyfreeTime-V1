Mass effect by: Bioware

Mass Effect is a sciencefiction shooter game set in the future milky way galaxy. You can either choose to be a male or female and become the main character of the game. Once you start playing you will become the first human to become a special agent of the alien council made to preserve peace in the milkyway galaxy.
