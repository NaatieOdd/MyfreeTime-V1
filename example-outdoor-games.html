Airsoft

Airsoft, also known as survival games (Japanese: サバイバルゲーム, romanized: sabaibaru gēmu) in Japan where it was first popularized, is a team-based shooting game in which participants eliminate opposing players out of play by tagging them with spherical plastic projectiles shot from low-power toy airguns called airsoft guns.
