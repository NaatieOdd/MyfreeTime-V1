Game of thrones by: HBO

Game of thrones is an on screen adaption of "a song of ice and fire" by George R.R. Martin. It is a story in a medieval fantasy setting. following multiple houses of families fighting to become the most powerfull and rule the iron throne, hence the name Game of Thrones. But whilst all this is happening a dangerous threat from the north arrives. and in the series you can find out how it ends.
