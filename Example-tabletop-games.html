Warhammer 40k by:Games Workshop

Warhammer 40k is a scifi table top game based in the 41st millenium. You can choose from all sorts of factions to play including humans. The game has a point based system to calculate even matches. And has in depth shooting mechanics and wounding mechanics through the use of dice.

For more info you can check out the wikipedia page.
